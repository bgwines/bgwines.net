reverse-scrabble

reverse-scrabble is a program to compute all possible histories leading to a given scrabble board state and set of player scores. It works by performing brute-force recursion, branching out as it tries each possible turn at each point.

It's my largest program in terms of volume of code, and was written in C++, in December 2012. To access the code, click here.
