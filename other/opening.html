Stanford Viennese Ball Opening Committee

The Stanford Viennese Ball Opening Committee is a ballroom dance group that performs two choreographies - a waltz and a polka - to open the annual Stanford Viennese Ball. It's existed for over thirty years. I've been performing with them for the past two years. Here are the dances:
