Stanford Viennese Ball Opening Committee

The Stanford Viennese Ball Opening Committee is a ballroom dance group that performs two choreographies - a waltz and a polka - to open the annual Stanford Viennese Ball. It's existed for over thirty years. I've been performing with them since 2012. Here are the dances:
   2014 Polka
   2014 Waltz
   2013 Polka
   2013 Waltz
   2012 Polka
   2012 Waltz
